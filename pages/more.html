What is weather?

The state of the air and atmosphere at a particular time and place. It can also be said that it is the wind and other outside conditions (such as rain, cloudiness, etc) at a particular time and place. Here you can actually know more than this.   • Descriptions and many more...

What is temperature?

This is a measurement that indicates how hot or cold somethin is. It can also be said that it is the measurement in degrees showing the heat of something (such as air or water). This measurements can be in Celius, Fahrenheit, Rankine, etc.

What is wind speed?

Wind is the natural movement of air of any velocity, especially the earth's air or the gas surrounding a planet in natural motion horizontally. Speed on the other hand is the rate at which something happens or is done. Together, wind speed refers to the rate at which the natural movement of air happens. This is measured in m/s(meters per second)

What is humidity

Humidity is the defined as the moisture in the air or the amount of moisture in the air. And moisture on the other hand is a small amount of a liquid (such as water) that makes something wet or moist. This is mearsured in percentage(%)
